Public bandwidth policy
Rate-limited, not unlimited

bcnode.io is operated as independent COSIGO Bitcoin infrastructure. The node is public, but bandwidth, uptime, monitoring, and maintenance are not free.

Historical block serving is rate-limited for sustainability. Stable Bitcoin peers that need higher bandwidth may request operator-verified peer review by providing their node IP address. Support does not buy consensus influence, transaction priority, private RPC access, or guaranteed uptime.
